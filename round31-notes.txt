Round 31 Investigation Notes
=============================

Issue 1: Employment Cost discrepancy
- Thomas Brown payroll totalEmploymentCost = GBP 7,949.78
- Invoice shows EUR 9,280.02 because customer (EuroSoft Solutions) settles in EUR
- GBP 7,949.78 × 1.167330 (GBP→EUR rate) = EUR 9,280.02
- This is CORRECT behavior. The invoice is in EUR, not GBP.
- The invoice item shows localAmount = 7,949.78 GBP and amount = 9,280.02 EUR
- Need to make this clearer in the UI (show local amount alongside converted amount)
